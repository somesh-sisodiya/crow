THIRSTY CROW

once oupon a time there was a thirsty crow.

He search for water every where.

At last he saw a water plot near a well

He look inside the plot.

There was very little water in the plot

The crow saw some stones nearby.

He thought of a plan.

He picked up some stones.

He put them one by one into yhe pot.

Water level in the pot came up.

The crow drank the water and flew away happily
